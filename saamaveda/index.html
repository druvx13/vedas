The Sāmaveda
English Translation (Griffith 1895)

Welcome to the electronic text of Ralph T.H. Griffith's English translation of the Sāmaveda, together with a popular commentary (1895).

The translation is divided into two parts – Part I (Purvarcika) containing 6 books, and Part II (Uttararcika) containing 9 books. A general introduction is also included.

If you find any error in the text, please report it to virenmir <at> gmail.com.

(All files are plain UTF-8 text; they may be freely searched and quoted.)
