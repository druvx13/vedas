The Atharvaveda
English Translation

Welcome to the electronic text of Maurice Bloomfield's English translation of the Atharvaveda (Sacred Books of the East, Volume 42, 1897), together with extracts from the ritual books and commentaries.

The translation is divided into ten logical sections corresponding to the major thematic groupings of the hymns. Each file (00.txt to 10.txt) contains the complete English rendering of the hymns belonging to that section, following Bloomfield's arrangement.

If you find any error in the text, please report it to the digital archive maintainers at virenmir <at> gmail.com.

(The files are in plain UTF-8 text format with Unix-style line endings; they may be freely searched, quoted, and processed with standard text tools.)
