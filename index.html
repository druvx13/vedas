The Vedas
English Translations

Welcome to the central index of English translations of the four Vedas and related Smṛti literature. Currently, complete translations of the Atharvaveda, the Sāmaveda, and the Manu Smṛti are available. The Ṛgveda and Yajurveda are in progress and will be added upon completion.

Each Veda has its own subdirectory containing the full text files and a detailed index. Click on the Veda name below to access its contents.

If you find any error in the text, please report it to virenmir <at> gmail.com.

Veda & Smṛti Index

Atharvaveda – Bloomfield translation (SBE 42, 1897) (Available)
Sāmaveda – Griffith translation (1895) (Available)
Manu Smṛti – Bühler translation (SBE 25, 1886) (Available)
Ṛgveda – Griffith translation (in progress) (Coming soon)
Yajurveda – various translations (in progress) (Coming soon)
