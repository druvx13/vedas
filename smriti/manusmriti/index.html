The Manu Smṛti
English Translation (Bühler)

Welcome to the electronic text of Georg Bühler's English translation of the Manu Smṛti (Laws of Manu), published in the Sacred Books of the East, Volume 25 (1886).

The text is divided into twelve chapters corresponding to the twelve books of the original. Each file contains the complete English rendering of the verses belonging to that chapter.

If you find any error in the text, please report it to virenmir <at> gmail.com.

(All files are in HTML format with UTF-8 encoding; they may be freely searched and quoted.)
